Software has always been a compromise between what one person wants and what's economical to build for many. App stores exist because the cost of building is high enough that you need a large audience to justify it. You download someone else's vision of a Pomodoro timer, with their preferences baked in, their monetization strategy attached. That logic is starting to break down. When a personalized app costs less than a coffee and takes minutes to describe, you stop downloading. You just ask AI to build.

What will this mean for the future of software? Maybe it will be generated on the fly, while you navigate and talk to your computer. You don't buy, download or install apps anymore. You just ask for what you want, and it appears.
