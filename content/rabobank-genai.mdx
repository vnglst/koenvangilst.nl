With a degree in philosophy and a lifelong passion for technology, my career has evolved from hands-on coding to leadership. Now, as Tech Lead GenAI at Rabobank, I'm driven by a clear mission: building a better bank by fighting complexity and applying GenAI where it truly adds value.

In this role, I focus on several key areas:

- **Experimenting with GenAI**: I evaluate the technical feasibility of new concepts through early-stage experiments and hands-on testing.
- **Building Strong Teams**: I recruit talented engineers and create supportive environments where teams can thrive and innovate.
- **Securing Resources**: I identify and obtain the technical resources needed to turn innovative ideas into reality.
- **Cross-Functional Collaboration**: Working closely with business lines and technical stakeholders ensures our strategies align with product goals.
- **Innovation Pipeline**: I guide projects from initial experimentation through to successful MVP development.

Central to my approach is fostering an engineering culture that values craftsmanship, technical expertise, and continuous learning. By creating environments where teams feel empowered to use the right technological solutions, I help Rabobank innovate effectively with AI while maintaining our focus on practical, valuable applications.
